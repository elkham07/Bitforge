Frequently Asked
Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. A simple web platform typically takes 2-3 months, while comprehensive mobile and web solutions can take 4-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure? +

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer agreements. Our starter packages begin at $5,000, with enterprise solutions customized to your specific needs and budget.

Do you provide ongoing support? +

Yes! All projects include a warranty period (3-12 months depending on the package), and we offer ongoing support and maintenance plans to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally.

Can you work with our existing systems? +

Absolutely. We specialize in integrating with existing systems and can work with your current technology stack. We'll assess your infrastructure during the discovery phase and recommend the best approach.

What industries do you serve? +

While we specialize in sports and coaching, we work with various industries including fitness, wellness, education, and healthcare. Our solutions are adaptable to any business that needs client management and engagement tools.
