ABOUT
NEURAL FORGE

We're on a mission to revolutionize coaching through innovative technology and human-centered design.

Our
Story

Founded in 2022, Neural Forge emerged from a simple observation: coaches were spending more time on administrative tasks than on what they do best – transforming lives through coaching.

Our founders, a team of tech enthusiasts and sports professionals, came together with a shared vision: create digital tools that automate the mundane so coaches can focus on the meaningful.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of coaching businesses worldwide, helping them scale their impact while maintaining the personal touch that makes their coaching special.
